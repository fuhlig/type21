Showcase

The Art of the Title is an excellent online resource dedicated to title design. Here are some examples of great title sequence with focus on typography

This service doesn't allow embedding so the videos can only be watched externally

the number 23

The typewritter typeface sets the mood for this mystery thriller and features historic dates and calculations around the number 23. Only text, blood spatters, fast cameramovement/cuts and music used in this. by the way: the movie is worth watching aswell!

mentiras piadosas

The text doesn't act as a seperate layer so display information. It is rather integrated and blends very well into the scene - especially with great use of light and shadowcasting.

Enter The Void

Enter the Void's title sequence is just like the movie - fast paces, light and color intense. The visual overdose is more like a preperation than an introduction for the viewer of what is coming.

Catch me if you can

Another great example of text integrated into the scene. The text is not only non distractive but part of the scenes and partly even make up shapes
